About Me

Hi! I'm João, a product designer passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences. I specialize in UI/UX design, design systems, and bringing ideas to life through thoughtful, user-centered design.

I’m a designer with a background in Multimedia Art, an Erasmus experience in England, and a Master’s in Design Management. I worked as the only junior designer at Real Hotels Group, creating digital and print work for multiple assets of the brand. I recently completed a Post-Graduate in Digital Experience Design, where I focused fully on UX.

When I'm not designing, you can find me reading a book, playing video games, or watching a movie.
