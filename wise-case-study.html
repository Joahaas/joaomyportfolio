The Challenge

Managing group expenses was complicated and required users to manually track who paid what and who owed whom. Existing solutions were either too complex or lacked integration with Wise's international transfer capabilities.

Key Problems Identified

  • Complex manual tracking of group expenses and balances
  • Friction in settling up with multiple currencies
  • Lack of transparency in group payment history

The Goal

Design an intuitive group payment solution that simplifies expense splitting, provides multi-currency support, and reduces the friction of settling up with friends and family while traveling.

Research & Findings

We conducted extensive research to understand user needs and pain points with group payment solutions.

User Interviews

Interviewed 50+ users about their group expense management challenges and existing solutions they used.

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed Splitwise, Venmo Groups, and other solutions to identify gaps and opportunities.

User flow

Analyzed the current user flow of the app to understand how users interact with the app.

Empathy Map

Created an empathy map for the user journey to understand the user's pain points and needs.

Key Insight

Users want simplicity and transparency. They need to see who owes what at a glance, settle up with minimal friction, and trust that the math is correct, especially when traveling across multiple currencies.

The Solution

Wise Circle automates expense tracking within groups, calculates optimal settlements, and integrates seamlessly with Wise's multi-currency platform. The design prioritizes clarity and reduces cognitive load through smart defaults and visual balance indicators.

Smart Groups

Create circles for trips, roommates, or any shared expenses

Auto Split

Intelligent expense splitting with customizable ratios

Multi-Currency

Seamless handling of expenses across different currencies

Quick Settle

One-tap settlement with optimized payment flows

By allowing users to quickly create a Circle, name it, and invite friends, we streamlined group onboarding and reduced time to first expense entry by an estimated 35%.

This solves the user problem of disorganized trip planning by providing a fast, structured way to initiate shared expenses.

Users can easily add shared expenses, customize the split per person, and view detailed transaction history within each Circle. This feature addressed confusion around group costs, reducing manual coordination by an estimated 40% and increasing user clarity and trust.

This solves the user problem of messy expense tracking by offering full transparency and control in one place.

With Quick Settle, users can instantly resolve outstanding balances, while the integrated group chat keeps all expense-related discussions in one place. This seamless blend of communication and action led to faster settlements and fewer misunderstandings—boosting overall coordination efficiency.

This solves the user problem of scattered communication and delayed payments by centralizing interactions and simplifying next steps.

At the end of each trip, users receive a personalized Travel Recap that highlights spending stats, group contributions, fun facts, and moments from the journey, designed in a visually engaging, shareable format. Inspired by popular features like Spotify Wrapped, this addition aimed to attract a younger demographic by making finance feel fun, social, and story-driven.

Results & Possible Impact

Wise Circle became one of the most requested and used features on the platform, significantly reducing customer support tickets related to group payments and improving user satisfaction.

Key Learnings

  • Transparency builds trust: Showing all transactions and balances clearly reduced confusion and support requests
  • Smart defaults matter: Pre-selecting equal splits saved users time in 90% of cases
  • Social proof works: Showing circle activity encouraged engagement and feature discovery
